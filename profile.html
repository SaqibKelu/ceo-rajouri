Office of the Chief Education Officer, Rajouri

Brief History

The Office of the Chief Education Officer (CEO), Rajouri has been playing a pivotal role in the development and administration of school education in the district. Since its inception, the department has been responsible for planning, implementing, and monitoring educational policies and programs of the Government in both urban and rural areas.

Over the years, the department has expanded its reach to ensure access to quality education for every child in the district, with special focus on infrastructure development, teacher capacity building, student welfare schemes, and digital transformation of schools.
